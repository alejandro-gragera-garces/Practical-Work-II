Fundamentals of Computer Engineering is an introductory subject that gives both an academic perspective that sets expectations for the rest of the degree. It focuses on teaching the basic concepts and applications of a computer engineers introducing a professional perspective.

The subject is divided in two halves, a theoretical half (reflected with tests and exams) and a practical half (Activities I, II, III, IV and Practical Works 1 & 2). An estimated 75 hours is expected for each half

OBJECTIVES:

  • Expand student's view of Computer Engineering to an international scale
  • Gain knowledge of the history behind the developement of computer systems and their components
  • Identify trends in the field of computer engineering
  • Developing creative problem-solving skills and applying them to reality
