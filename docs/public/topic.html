What are Avionics?

Avionics are the electronic systems integrated (most relevantly) in airoplanes and satelites that perform the translations and calculations for the pilot of data transmitted from ground-based navigation systems to carry out Instrument assisted flights or orbits .

These systems include navigation, communication, flight control, and monitoring equipment and assist pilots in all Flight Information Regions (FIR) with the proper ground based infrastructure (excludes ANTARTICA, AFRICA ..etc.).

The popular saying of how flights are the safest transport largely stems from the fact that Instrument assisted flights don't depend on how visible certain landmarks are to the pilot in bad weather

Why have I chosen Avionics?

Since October 2025 I have been studying for my Air Trafic Control Examinations and I was very pleasently surprised with the evolution of the software that is currently in use to direct our planes over the past decades.

Pilots are still esential for flights, but pressure has been gradually taken off their shoulders even in regions where the hardware is stuck in past generations.

I consider that adjusting software to fit with outdated hardware to execute long distanced flights si impressive in our field and can be applied to many other industries
